Our Services

Firm Strategy

We help companies strengthen their strategy-development processes, make better decisions, and implement their strategy. We assist with solutions to specific strategy issues or help design a whole new approach to strategic positioning and strategy implementation.

Legal Intellectual Property and Regulatory Approval

We help companies with intellectual property strategy and understanding regulatory approval processes and requirements.

Venture Funding VCs, Angels, and Grant Writing

We advise companies on how to raise capital in a way that is appropriate for the companyâ€™s stage of growth, goals, and challenges. We help companies determine the right mix of debt and equity financing and the amount of financing needed at each stage of growth to achieve company goals.

Financial Modeling

We help companies develop financial modeling for decision making and performing financial analysis for fund raising, valuation, budgeting and forecasting, resource allocation, and financial statement analysis.

Engineering R&D

We advise companies on new product development from customer needs assessment to product design, prototyping, and pilot testing.

Supply Chain and Manufacturing

We help companies find supply chain partners for sourcing materials, components, and services. We also advise on production planning and prototype, pilot, and scaling manufacturing.
